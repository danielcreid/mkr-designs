Melanie + Daniel Wedding

Branding + Print design

For my wedding, I wanted to create a cohesive brand to be used on all of our design elements such as invitations, ceremony programs, table decorations, and the website. I decided to use a venn diagram icon based on the idea of two lives becoming one. I also used two turtles as another design element after my husband and I misunderstood each other hearing "love is a turtle" instead of "love is eternal."
