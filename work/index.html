Orange Cupboard Jelly Company

Branding + Package Design

Orange Cupboard Jelly Company was started by my husband's aunt, Kathy. The name for the company started with the cupboard Kathy restored and painted orange after it was passed down from her mother. It became a cupboard that Kathy stored all of her jelly in, which stemmed the name Orange Cupboard Jelly Company. The logo was inspired by a drawing on one of the cupboard's chalkboard doors. Little 8oz jars of jelly were then given as favors at my wedding.
