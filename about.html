Hello! My name is Melanie.

I started my career as a photographer, but quickly realized that design was a much more interesting and fulfilling career path. I enjoy the creative process, having "a-ha!" moments, and making a difference in people's lives through design. Over the years, I found myself taking on more branding projects. I really enjoy them and want to pursue more in the future.

I have a wonderful husband, Daniel, and we have 3 plant children: an avocado tree and two house plants named Planty and Planty Jr. When I'm not working, I love to crochet, play video games, or bake delicious treats. I will always love the St. Louis Cardinals, toasted ravioli, and gooey butter cake, but I have fallen for Milwaukee's fried cheese curds and craft beer.

I'm currently working as a designer at Kohl's Corporation in Milwaukee, Wisconsin.
